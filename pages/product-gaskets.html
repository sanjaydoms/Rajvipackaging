Why Choose Rajvi
Precision Sealing
Die-cut and CNC-machined to exact tolerances, forming consistent seals against dust, air and moisture ingress.
Vibration Control
Compressible foam structure absorbs and dampens mechanical vibration between mating surfaces.
Thermal Insulation

Custom Profiles
