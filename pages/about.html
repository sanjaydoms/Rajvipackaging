Our Story

Built From Industrial Expertise, Driven By Customization

Rajvi Packaging was founded with a clear objective: to build a packaging company that treats every client's product as a unique engineering challenge. Not a catalogue item, but a protection problem to solve.

We specialize in creating packaging systems tailored to product protection, movement, storage and handling requirements. With fully integrated in-house capabilities — from design and development to production — we deliver solutions that combine customization, flexibility and deep industry understanding.

Today, our 27,000 sq.ft facility houses precision CNC cutting, lamination lines, fabrication and full assembly — operated by 80+ trained professionals working across 10+ product categories. One partner. Every packaging need.
