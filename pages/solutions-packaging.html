Overview

Packaging Solutions

Our complete packaging solutions span foam, PP, air bubble, protective and rigid categories — each manufactured to specification, tested for performance, and delivered on time for your operational requirements. From single-component protective inserts to complete multi-material packaging systems.

How We Work

Process Timeline

01
Requirement Analysis
We assess product protection requirements, transit conditions, volume and operational environment before recommending the most effective solution approach.
02
Concept Development
Solution concepts are developed that address protection requirements within cost and operational targets — and presented for customer evaluation.
03
Material Selection
The right material combination is selected based on protection type, weight, moisture exposure, reusability needs and total cost of ownership.
04
Prototype
Physical samples are produced and evaluated with the customer before production is confirmed — eliminating downstream surprises.
05
Validation
Prototypes are validated against performance requirements and customer approvals secured before production release.
06
Production
In-house production using CNC cutting, lamination, thermoforming and assembly lines with quality inspection at each stage.
Where We Apply It

Industries & Applications

Automotive
Where
PP returnable systems for component supply chains, foam inserts for precision parts, FLC pallets for bulk logistics.
Why
High-volume, repeatable logistics demand packaging that performs consistently across hundreds of use cycles without degradation.
Solves
Single-use packaging cost, component damage in transit, and empty return packaging logistics costs.
Electronics OEM
Where
ESD-safe packaging inserts for PCBs and electronic assemblies, anti-static bubble pouches for components.
Why
Electronic components require multi-layer protection — physical, electrostatic and moisture — in a single solution.
Solves
ESD failures, transit damage and compliance gaps in electronic component packaging.
Logistics & E-Commerce
Where
Bubble rolls, EPE foam, void fill and corrugated systems for fulfilment centre operations.
Why
High throughput operations require packaging that is fast to apply, consistent in protection and economical at volume.
Solves
Delivery damage rates, return rates and packaging material cost efficiency across fulfilment operations.
White Goods & FMCG
Where
Foam corner protectors, surface protection laminates, corrugated outer packaging for appliance distribution.
Why
Large, heavy products with finished surfaces require protection that absorbs handling impacts without marking.
Solves
Transit damage, surface marking on finished appliances and warranty claims from delivery-related damage.
