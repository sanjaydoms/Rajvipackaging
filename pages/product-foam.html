FOAM &FOAM BUFFERS

Rajvi Packaging manufactures engineered foam solutions designed to protect products against impact, vibration, compression and handling damage throughout storage, transportation and industrial operations.

Product Variants — click to preview
PU Foam
Soft, flexible cushioning foam for shock absorption and delicate product protection. Excellent impact resistance against vibration and transit damage.
EPE Foam
Lightweight closed-cell protective foam with superior cushioning and moisture resistance. Ideal for packaging and handling applications.
XLPE Foam
High-performance industrial foam with superior durability, insulation and surface finish. Used for precision protection and thermal insulation.
EPDM & ESD Foam
Specialized foam for sensitive and static-sensitive environments. Protects electronic components from electrostatic discharge.
