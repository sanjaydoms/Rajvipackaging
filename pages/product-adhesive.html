ADHESIVELAMINATION

Adhesive lamination combines materials to enhance protection, durability and functionality. Enables multi-layer protective solutions combining foam, film, board and fabric layers into integrated packaging systems.

Foam + Film Laminate for Appliance Surface Protection
A home appliance manufacturer required scratch-resistant surface protection for pre-assembled panels during assembly line handling and transit. Adhesive-laminated PE film on EPE foam delivered a peel-clean protective layer with zero adhesive residue.

An electronics OEM required packaging inserts combining structural rigidity, ESD protection and surface cushioning in one component. A three-layer adhesive lamination of ESD film, EPE foam and board provided all three.
