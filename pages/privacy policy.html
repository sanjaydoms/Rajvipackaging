POLICY

Effective Date: June 2026

Rajvi Packaging ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or communicate with us regarding our products and services. By accessing or using this website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Section 01

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our website or contact us:

Personal Information

  • Full Name
  • Company Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Business Address (if provided)
  • Product Enquiry Details & Packaging Requirements
  • Any information you voluntarily submit through our contact forms or email communications

Automatically Collected Information

When you browse our website, certain technical information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP Address, Browser Type & Operating System
  • Device Information
  • Pages Visited & Time Spent on the Website
  • Referring Website & General Usage Data

This information helps us improve our website performance and user experience.

Section 02

How We Use Your Information

The information collected may be used for the following purposes:

  • Responding to product and business enquiries
  • Providing quotations and product information
  • Communicating regarding packaging solutions and services
  • Improving our website functionality and customer experience
  • Managing customer relationships and maintaining internal business records
  • Complying with applicable legal obligations
Section 03

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and understand website usage. Cookies help us improve website performance, remember user preferences, analyse visitor traffic, and enhance website security.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, certain website features may not function properly as a result.

Section 04

Sharing of Information

Rajvi Packaging respects your privacy and does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only when necessary:

  • With trusted service providers assisting in website operation or business communication
  • When required by applicable law or legal process
  • To protect our legal rights, property, or safety
Section 05

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

Although we strive to protect your information, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Section 06

Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites for your convenience. Rajvi Packaging is not responsible for the privacy practices, policies, or content of external websites. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Section 07

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfil business purposes, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Section 08

Your Rights

You may request to access your personal information, correct inaccurate information, update your contact details, or request deletion of your personal information where permitted by law.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
