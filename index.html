What We Do

COMPLETE
SOLUTIONS

01 Design Solutions +

From product analysis to production-ready documentation — we engineer packaging precisely matched to your product's geometry, weight, fragility and handling requirements. CAD-based structural design, material selection, prototyping and full validation before any production commitment.

02 Packaging Solutions +

Our complete packaging range spans foam, PP corrugated, air bubble, adhesive lamination, FLC pallets and specialty tapes — all manufactured in-house to specification and tested for performance. One partner covering every packaging category, at any volume.

03 Manufacturing Solutions +

CNC precision cutting, adhesive lamination, die cutting, thermoforming and assembly — all under one 27,000 sq.ft roof with 80+ trained professionals. Consistent quality from prototype through high-volume production, with inspection at every stage before dispatch.
